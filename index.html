FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTION

Can I log in to different game platforms with the ROG Ally?

Yes, it supports Xbox Game Pass, Steam, Epic Games, Ubisoft Connect, EA App, and GOG Galaxy.

Does the ROG Ally support external storage?

Yes, it has a UHS-II microSD card slot for expanding storage.

What is the battery life of the ROG Ally?

It lasts around 2-4 hours, depending on usage.

Can I upgrade the RAM on the ROG Ally?

No, the RAM is soldered and cannot be upgraded.

Can the ROG Ally support the XG Mobile external GPU?

Yes, but the ROG Ally X variant does not support it.

What should I do if the screen is flickering or freezing?

Update GPU drivers and Windows or adjust the refresh rate.

How do I capture gameplay footage on the ROG Ally?

Hold M1/M2 + A for screenshots or M1/M2 + Y for video.

Can I connect the ROG Ally to a TV?

Yes, using the Gaming Charger Dock.
